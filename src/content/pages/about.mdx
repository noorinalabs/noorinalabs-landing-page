## Our Mission

NoorinALabs exists to serve the Islamic scholarly tradition through thoughtful
technology. We build open, accessible tools that help researchers, students, and
communities engage with classical sources in new ways — without replacing the
human scholarship that gives those sources meaning.

## What We Believe

- **Scholarship first.** Technology should amplify scholarly work, not
  substitute for it. Every tool we build is grounded in published academic
  sources and reviewed by domain experts.
- **Open by default.** Our code, data pipelines, and research are open source.
  Transparency builds trust and invites collaboration.
- **Accessible to all.** Whether you are a seasoned researcher or a first-time
  learner, our tools should meet you where you are. We design for clarity,
  performance, and inclusivity.
- **Respectful of tradition.** We approach Islamic sciences with the reverence
  they deserve. Our tone is scholarly but welcoming, precise but never
  exclusionary.

## What We're Building

Our first project, the **Isnad Graph**, applies graph computation to the hadith
sciences — making chains of narration explorable and searchable at scale. More
projects are on the way, each rooted in the same principles: serve scholarship,
stay open, remain respectful.

## Get Involved

NoorinALabs is a community effort. If you are a scholar, developer, designer, or
simply someone who cares about preserving and sharing Islamic knowledge, we would
love to hear from you.
