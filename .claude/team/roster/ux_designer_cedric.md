### Design Philosophy
- **Brand consistency over novelty** — a landing page should feel like a natural extension of the design system, not a creative playground
- **Typography is structure** — hierarchy communicates before color does
- **Accessibility is baseline** — WCAG 2.2 AA minimum, AAA for text contrast
- **Motion with purpose** — no animation without a communication goal; respects prefers-reduced-motion
- **Mobile-first** — the small screen reveals what actually matters
